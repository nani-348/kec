# Water Balance & Energy Images

This directory contains high-quality images for the Water Balance & Energy section.

## Required Images

### 1. kada-region-map.jpg
- **Description**: KADA Region Administrative Map showing 4 mandals
- **Content**: Map with KADA boundary, micro basin boundary, mandal boundary, village boundary, and streams
- **Dimensions**: Recommended 3840x2160 (4K) or higher
- **Source**: First uploaded image (KADA Kuppam map)

### 2. mi-tanks-storage.jpg
- **Description**: MI Tanks Storage Comparison Chart
- **Content**: Bar chart showing Capacity (mcft) vs Current Storage (mcft) for all 4 mandals
- **Dimensions**: Recommended 3840x2160 (4K) or higher
- **Source**: Second uploaded image (MI Tanks Storage chart)

### 3. filling-status.jpg
- **Description**: Tank Filling Status Distribution
- **Content**: Bar chart showing number of tanks at different fill levels (100%, 75%, 50%, 25%, 0%)
- **Dimensions**: Recommended 3840x2160 (4K) or higher
- **Source**: Third uploaded image (Filling Status chart)

## Image Specifications

- **Format**: JPEG (high quality, 90-95% compression)
- **Resolution**: 4K (3840x2160) or higher for best magnification experience
- **Color Space**: sRGB
- **File Size**: Optimize for web (typically 500KB - 2MB per image)

## Usage

These images are used in the MagnifyingImageViewer component which allows users to:
- Click to expand in a modal
- Hover to magnify specific areas
- View high-resolution details
